PROJECT PORTOFOLIO

Coastal Engineering

Hydrodynamic Modelling of Kuta Beach

Objective: To analyze wave deformation and sediment transport along the coastline.

Method: Numerical simulation using MIKE 21 HD and analysis of 50 years of wave data (2025–2076).

Results: Current distribution maps and coastal protection recommendations (breakwater/groin).

WATER RESOURCES ENGINEERING

River Hydrology Analysis

Objective: To analyze river flow behavior for flood control purposes.

Method: Analysis of rainfall and river discharge data.

Results: Identification of flow patterns and peak discharge for flood mitigation planning.

Geospatial Analysis

Topographic Survey Using Drone (ITS Area)

Objective: To collect topographic data across the ITS area using drone-based surveying methods.

Method: Aerial survey using drone imagery combined with GIS processing for spatial data extraction and analysis.

Results: Generation of a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) and topographic map for supporting engineering and spatial analysis within ITS area.

Civil Design

Coastal Shoreline Analysis

Objective: To analyze shoreline changes due to erosion and sedimentation processes for coastal protection purposes.

Method: Shoreline condition analysis using spatial data and hydrodynamic modelling, supported by AutoCAD Civil 3D.

Results: Identification of erosion and sedimentation zones along the coastline, along with recommendations for coastal protection systems such as seawalls and breakwaters.
